Palawan

Palawan is a big island west of the Philippines. Because of the white sand beaches, amazing islands, clear blue waters, a diverse variety of marine life, and breathtaking views the island has. It is consistently ranked as one of the best islands in the world. The island has a lot of cities, to the north there's El Nido and in the middle, as well as the capital, there's Puerto Princesa.



What to do?

There are many acitivies to do in Palawan, such as island hopping, diving and snorkeling, relaxing in the beach, viewing and taking pictures of multiple sites around the island, going on a hike on the mountains, and many more.
